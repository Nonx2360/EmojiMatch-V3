## üéÆ How to Play

1.  **Setup**: Enter the number of players and their names. Select your preferred grid size.
2.  **Start**: Click "Start Game" to enter the game arena.
3.  **Turn**: Click a card to reveal the emoji. Click a second card to try and match it.
    *   **Match**: If they match, you get a point, and the cards stay face up.
    *   **Miss**: If they don't match, they flip back over, and it's the next player's turn.
4.  **Win**: The player with the most pairs when all cards are matched wins!
